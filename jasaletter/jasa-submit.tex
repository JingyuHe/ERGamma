\opening{Dear Editor,}

Please find enclosed the paper \emph{"Bayesian Inference for Gamma Models"} that we would like to submit to 
\emph{JASA Theory and Methods}. 

The paper introduces a new family of distributions named exponential reciprocal gamma (ERG), which allows a data augmentation scheme for models that include gamma functions. This strategy applies to many popular statistical or machine learning models including, but not limited to, multinomial-Dirichlet, negative binomial regression, Poisson-Gamma hierarchical models, extreme value models, etc. This paper also develops scalable EM and MCMC algorithms for inference and demonstrates our methodology on various examples.

This paper fits to the area of non-parametric Bayes and Gamma hierarchical models. We would recommend Lijoi (Bocconi) for possible AE, and possible reviewers Aktekin (U of New Hampshire) or Mallick (Texas A\&M).
